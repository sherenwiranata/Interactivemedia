One of the hardest but most interesting parts was implementing background music with the Play Music button. I experimented with loadSound() and .loop() from p5.js:. Key lesson learned: Browsers block autoplay sound unless there’s a user interaction. I reflected back on my earlier Kusama sketch and realised that sound needs to be triggered by user input.I also studied the Healing Forest p5 sketch and saw how they delayed sound loading until a click occurred. This inspired me to add a clear toggle interaction.
