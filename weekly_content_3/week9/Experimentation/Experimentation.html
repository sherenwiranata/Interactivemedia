WEEPER'S GLASSES

Weeper’s Glasses are wearable emotion enhancers for those moments when the tears just won’t come. Powered by Arduino, a button-activated servo lid releases a hint of onion juice near the eyes, triggering a gentle, involuntary cry. Sometimes, when life gets heavy, it’s easier to let it all out, but what if you can’t? With Weeper’s Glasses, you can join the weepers, even when your own tears are stuck inside. They come in the form of tinted sunglasses, designed to discreetly mask your post-cry puffiness, so you can mourn, release, or simply feel in private, even in public.
